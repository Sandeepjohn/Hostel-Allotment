Mess Rule

  • All the students of the hostel should take their food in the allotted mess only.
  • Change of mess is allowed once in a month. The mess change is allowed in the end of the previous month so that he/she can dine from the beginning of the next month..
  • A separate Mess Card will be issued to each hostel block student. The identity card is to be produced at the dining hall, at the time of breakfast, lunch, tea and dinner, failing which service in the dining hall is liable to be refused, for want of identification.
  • The hostel kitchen is strictly "Out of Bounds" for the students/parents/guardians.
  • Sick person will be served mess food in the room, only if the student registers their name with the Warden.
